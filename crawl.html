EPISODE 0.5
Poor Life-Choices


It is a dark time for the students. The Institute, led by their cruel taskmaster Emperor Juanmatine and his iron right hand, Darth Darragh have demanded that all must submit a project demonstrating their competence with a range of front-end technologies. But there is a glimmer of hope for the embattled students. A pair of mystical powers known as "CSS" and "HTML" have recently emerged. With careful training, perhaps the students will be able wield these powers for good...
